Lasagna

Garfield is coming for you.

Lasagna is an Italian dish made of pasta sheets layered with fillings and baked. The dish can be made with a variety of ingredients, including meat, cheese, vegetables, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover the foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
